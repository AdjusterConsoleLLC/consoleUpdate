    BaseConsole - Tutorial

    Clip button Clicking these buttons instantly copies your text to your clipboard.
    Solo button Clicking these buttons sends your preset text to the main textarea.
    PopNotes Click a PopNote button to show your stored text in a window. Click the button again to hide the window.
    Slides Clicking a slide button will display three additional buttons. Buttons automatically move off-screen when one is selected or after a preset amount of time.
    Button Sets Parent button can spawn up to 4 child buttons. Child buttons can be Clip, Solo, or generate up to 4 more child buttons each. Grandchild buttons are always Clip or Solo.
